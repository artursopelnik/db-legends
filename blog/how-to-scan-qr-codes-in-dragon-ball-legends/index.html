How to Scan Friend QR Codes in Dragon Ball Legends (Step by Step)

Updated July 20, 2026 · DBL QR Blog

During anniversary and special events, Dragon Ball Legends lets you scan your friends' QR codes to collect Dragon Balls together – and once you've gathered all seven, you can summon Shenron for rewards. This guide walks you through the whole process: finding the scanner, getting scannable codes from your friends, and what to do when you can't meet in person.

Where is the QR scanner in Dragon Ball Legends?

The scanner is part of the friends menu:

  1. Open Dragon Ball Legends and tap Menu.
  2. Go to Friends.
  3. Select Scan QR code (the option appears while a scan event is running).
  4. Point your camera at your friend's QR code.

Each unique friend you scan gives you a Dragon Ball. Scanning the same person twice doesn't award a second one, so you'll need several different friends to complete the set of seven.

The problem: you can't scan your own screen

The catch is that the QR code is shown inside the game – on your friend's phone. If your friends aren't sitting next to you, you can't scan anything. That's exactly what a QR generator solves: your friends send you their friend codes as plain text, and you turn each code into a scannable QR image on a second screen.

With the free DBL QR generator it works like this:

  1. Ask your friends for their friend codes (8–10 letters and numbers).
  2. Paste each code into the generator – it instantly renders a QR code.
  3. Display the QR codes on a laptop, tablet or second phone.
  4. Open the scanner in the game and scan them one by one.

Why "fresh" QR codes matter

Dragon Ball Legends only accepts QR codes with a recent timestamp. A code that was generated hours ago will be rejected by the scanner. Always regenerate the QR codes right before you scan – the generator has a Regenerate button that refreshes every saved code with a current timestamp in one tap.

Tips for a smooth scan session

  • Use two devices. Show the QR code on one screen and scan with the phone running the game.
  • Turn up the screen brightness on the device showing the code.
  • Scan several friends in a row. The generator keeps a whole list of codes, so you can go through them quickly.
  • Trade scans. Your friends need your code too – find yours under Menu → Friends and share it back.
